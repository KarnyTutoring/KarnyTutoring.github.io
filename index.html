Tutoring

Introductory High School Chemistry

I will help you understand the foundational concepts of high school chemistry, including atomic structure, chemical bonding, stoichiometry, and more. We will work at the pace you need to ensure your understanding and success.

AP Chemistry

I am well-versed in the requirements of the AP Chemistry exam and can help you prepare for success. I will work with you to understand concepts like kinetics, electrochemistry, and thermodynamics, and help you practice for the exam.

Honors Chemistry

Honors chemistry can be a challenging subject, but I will help every step of the way to give you the tools you need so you can excel in your studies. I will work with you to help you understand advanced concepts like thermodynamics, equilibrium, and acid-base chemistry.

College Chemistry

College-level chemistry can be daunting, especially if you struggled a bit in your high school course, but I am confident that I can help you succeed. I will work with you to revisit and expand on general chemistry topics, as well as help you grasp organic chemistry topics including functional groups, nomenclature, and reactions.

About Me

I am a retired chemistry teacher with more than 30 years of experience teaching chemistry in independent high schools and I now offer my expertise to students via online tutoring for all levels of general high school chemistry, Honors chemistry, AP Chemistry, or introductory college or organic chemistry. My goal is to help students overcome any anxiety they may have about the subject of chemistry by patiently explaining each topic and building their confidence level through guided practice. As the student articulates their thought process while solving problems, I can provide tools and strategies to help them achieve greater success in their chemistry courses and build a solid foundation for future scientific endeavors.
